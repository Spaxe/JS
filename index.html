Variable Names

In programming, names are called Identifiers.

In Javascript, identifiers can only have:

  • letters [A-Za-z]
  • numbers [0-9]
  • dollar signs [$]
  • underscores [_]

Scope

Variables have a defined lifespan. They become undefined when they "go out of scope".

Scopes are defined by functions.
